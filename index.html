01 · The problem

Remote work is permanent infrastructure on a fragile foundation.

The shift to distributed teams now relies on the most volatile infrastructure on earth: residential ISPs. When the last mile breaks, leadership loses confidence, customer experience plummets, and billable productivity stops.

!

The Blind Spot

Teams are distributed and hiring is borderless, but the office now depends on hardware organizations do not own or control.

✕

The Impact

Disconnections cause lost sales, burned leads, contract penalties, and stranded billable hours across the workforce.

⛨

The Business Risk

Network issues don't just break systems — they break leadership confidence in the entire remote operating model.

03 · Why it works

The illusion of speed tests vs. the reality of evidence.

A speed test is a snapshot. A real network problem is a pattern over time. Until you can show the pattern, you can't prove the problem.

The Illusion

Legacy Speed Test

Metric Focus
Bandwidth capacity at a single moment in time.
Timeframe
A 30-second snapshot. Misses intermittent flapping entirely.
Resolution
Confirms something is wrong. Result = guesswork.
The Reality

Distributed Monitoring

Metric Focus
Latency, packet loss, jitter, and MOS for VoIP quality.
Timeframe
24/7/365 timeline tracking. 34,560 data points per day, per agent.
Resolution
Identifies the exact failing hop. Result = objective evidence.
"Perspective is the difference between guessing and solving."
